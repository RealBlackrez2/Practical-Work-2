About the Movie

Jujutsu Kaisen: Hidden Inventory/Premature Death – The Movie is a 2025 anime film that serves as a recap of the first five episodes of Jujutsu Kaisen's second season. It premiered in Japanese theaters on March 30, 2025. GKIDS has produced an English dub of the film, which will premiere in North American theaters exclusively on July 16 and 17, 2025.
